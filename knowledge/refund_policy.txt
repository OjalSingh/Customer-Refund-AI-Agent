DUPLICATE CHARGE REFUND POLICY

Purpose:
To determine whether a customer is eligible for refund review due to duplicate charges.

Eligibility Requirements:

1. At least two successful transactions must exist.

2. Transactions must belong to the same merchant.

3. Transactions must be for the same amount.

4. Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the transaction.

5. The AI agent cannot directly approve or process refunds.

6. Eligible cases must be escalated to a human reviewer.

Rejection Conditions:

1. User account cannot be verified.

2. Transactions belong to different merchants.

3. Transaction amounts differ.

4. One or more transactions failed.

5. Insufficient transaction evidence.

Escalation Policy:

All eligible refund requests require human review before funds are returned.
